Aung San Suu Kyi is a Burmese politician, economist, peace activist, Civil Rights activist, diplomat and an author. She was born on 19th June, 1945. Her father, Aung San was a Burmese politician and revolutionary. He lived from 13 February, 1915 to 19 July 1947. Daw Suu's mother, Khin Kyi was a Burmese politician and diplomat. She lived from 16 April, 1912 to 27 December, 1988. Amay Suu or Mother Suu is now 75 years old and is now the current State Counsellor of Myanmar. She brought democracy to her country with non'violence. She is also the leader of the National League for Democracy in Burma and is a famous prisoner. Daw suu won the Nobel peace. prize on 14 October, 1991 for her non-violent struggle for democracy and human rights. She has won many other medals such as the sakharov prize, Congressional gold medal, The Wallenberg Medal and many more. Amay Suu used the Nobel Peace Prize, 1.3 million US Dollars to establish a health and education trust for the Burmese people. Aung Suu San Kyi did many great, noble deeds for her country.
